What Is Slow Down?

The Slow Down Language website is a phraseology-based tool where users can learn Japanese (or English) through a story in a meditative or serene state. Slow Down Language is a site where the narrator simultaneously tells a story in English and Japanese. One sentence is read in English then a translation of that sentence is read in Japanese. The reading will be slow and melodic, which is suitable for someone who is relaxing, meditating, or about to go to bed.
